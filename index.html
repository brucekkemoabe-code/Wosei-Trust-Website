Who We Are

A Trust Built on Women's Strength

WOSEI Trust — the Women Social Entrepreneurial Initiative — is a locally-rooted non-profit organisation dedicated to unlocking the transformative power of women in our communities. We believe that when a woman is empowered, her entire family and community thrives.

Through hands-on training, mentorship, and access to resources, we equip women with the skills they need to lead, earn, and inspire — whether in digital technology, traditional crafts, or community leadership.

Code of Conduct for Members

All members, beneficiaries, and participants of WOSEI Trust are expected to uphold our values and conduct themselves professionally. Review our code of conduct below:

  • 01

    Respect Fellow Participants: Treat all community members, beneficiaries, and peers with dignity, respect, and kindness.

  • 02

    Respect Trainers & Coordinators: Cooperate fully with instructors, program organizers, and team facilitators.

  • 03

    Protect Trust Property: Care for and responsibly use computers, tools, materials, and facilities provided during training.

  • 04

    Punctuality & Attendance: Arrive on time for training sessions and maintain regular attendance for program compliance.

  • 05

    Avoid Disruptive Behaviour: Ensure the learning environment remains positive, focused, and free from disturbance.

  • 06

    Zero Discrimination & Harassment: We maintain a strictly inclusive space. Any discrimination or harassment is unacceptable.

  • 07

    Use Skills Responsibly: Apply the digital, business, and craft skills learned for positive socio-economic advancement.

  • 08

    Promote Trust Objectives: Support the mission of WOSEI Trust to uplift women and build self-reliance across Botswana.

  • 09

    Professionalism: Uphold professional standards, honest representation, and active participation in program evaluations.

⚠️

Misconduct Policy: Any member found engaging in misconduct, theft, disruption, or violating these rules will face immediate suspension or termination of their participation.

🏘️

Community Engagement

WOSEI Trust identifies wards, settlements, and villages with high unemployment or poverty rates to target intervention. Prior to launching projects, we collaborate closely with local leaders: Chiefs (Dikgosi), Village Development Committees (VDCs), Councillors, MPs, and government departments to foster local ownership and transparency.

🤝

Branding Whizz Strategic Partnership

To ensure sustainability and operational continuity, WOSEI Trust collaborates with Branding Whizz ↗ for administration, coordination, and marketing of outreach projects. All revenues generated through Branding Whizz outreach efforts are managed transparently to support the expansion of our training initiatives.

🏺

Manufacturing & Livelihood Projects

We prioritize local resources and community participation to establish agricultural, textile, pottery, recycling, and food processing initiatives. The Trust retains a reasonable percentage of proceeds from large-scale projects to cover administration, equipment maintenance, and trainer remuneration.

🌱

Funding & Sustainability

We are a registered non-profit and do not operate for private gain. All funding received from donors, private sponsors, international agencies, and philanthropic organizations is used responsibly to advance community objectives.
